Volunteer with Root Causes

The Fresh Produce Program (FPP) is operated out of our Distribution Hub, 1925 Yearby Ave, where we store, pack, and deliver locally-sourced fresh fruits and veggies to our Durham neighbors in need.

1925 Yearby Ave, Durham, NC  · 

Click on any shift below to learn more and find a time that works best for you.

If you have any questions or need more information, email volunteers@rootcauses.org.

Delivery Driving

Pick up packed bags of fresh produce from our Distribution Hub and drive to community members across Durham. Deliveries are contactless — place the bag at the door, knock, and head to the next stop. A great way to directly connect food with neighbors in need.

Participant Check-In Calling

Call program participants to confirm delivery windows, check in on their needs, and make sure no one is missed. This shift can often be done remotely — perfect if you want to help from home.

Distribution Day Food Packing

Join us at the Hub to sort and pack fresh produce into bags ready for delivery. Work alongside other volunteers in an assembly-line style — high energy, team-driven, and incredibly impactful.
